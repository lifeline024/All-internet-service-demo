Synopsis

"Emergency" is an upcoming Bollywood film directed by and starring Kangana Ranaut. Set against the backdrop of the political turbulence in India during the Emergency period (1975-1977), the film focuses on the life of former Prime Minister Indira Gandhi. Kangana plays the role of Indira Gandhi, portraying her complex persona, challenges, and the political decisions made during this critical time in India's history. The film delves into themes of power, governance, and the impact of political decisions on ordinary people.
