Synopsis

"Mission Grey House" is an upcoming Indian Hindi-language suspense thriller film. The story follows Kabir Rathod, a young man determined to become a police officer. In his quest to achieve his goal, he confronts criminals and finds himself trapped in the mysterious Grey House, where he faces danger and uncovers dark secrets.
