Synopsis

"Azaad" is a Hindi-language period drama set in pre-Independence India. The film follows a family’s unwavering love for their loyal horse, and the moral dilemma faced by the clan’s head when the horse is accused of a crime. This action-packed drama takes viewers on a journey of justice, redemption, and honor.
